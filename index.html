About Me

Howdy! I'm Allie Clements, a first year Masters of Computer Science student at Texas A&M University. I graduated in June 2025 with a B.S. in Computer Science from Stanford University.

I’m a student interested in web development, design, and how technology can be more accessible for everyone.

Interests

Accessibility Interests

I care about accessibility because digital spaces should be usable by all people, regardless of ability.

I concentrate a lot on Front End design, User Interface, User Experience, and design principles & process within the HCI field as well. I've taken some classes that featured a little accessibility coursework, but I'm very excited to be taking a class that solely focuses on it.

I’m especially interested in screen reader usability, color contrast, and making interfaces easier to navigate.
